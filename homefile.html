Havmor Ice Cream

where happiness meets taste! Founded in 1944, Havmor has grown from a small family-run shop into one of India’s most loved ice cream brands. With a legacy of over 75 years, Havmor is known for crafting delicious, creamy, and innovative ice creams that bring smiles to millions of people every day.

Welcome to Havmor Ice Creams :

From classic flavors like vanilla and chocolate to unique innovations like paan, badam kulfi, and sundaes, Havmor blends tradition with modern taste. With over 150 varieties, there’s a scoop for everyone – kids, families, and ice cream lovers of all ages. Today, Havmor is part of the global Lotte Confectionery Group, expanding its delight beyond India and making its way to international markets.

At Havmor, we believe: Ice cream is not just a dessert, it’s an emotion. Every bite should deliver quality, joy, and unforgettable taste. Happiness tastes best when shared with loved ones. Whether it’s a family celebration, a festive occasion, or a late-night craving, Havmor Ice Creams will always be there to make life a little sweeter.
